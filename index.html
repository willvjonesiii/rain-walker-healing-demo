Hello! My name is Nikki Zalesak and I am the Founder of Rain Walker Healing. I am a Medical Massage Therapist, Certified Professional Hypnotist, Reiki Master, and Sound Healer. I strive to help my patients thrive physically, emotionally, and spiritually.

Inspiration for my work is rooted in overcoming a lifetime of intense ups and downs, as a sensitive being. By trusting my intuition I found the best methods and tools available to heal, expand, and thrive. Expect a wealth of knowledge, experience, and compassion for those searching for mind, body, and spirit health!

The name "Rain Walker" was given to me by a spiritual teacher in 2016. This came after witnessing an unexplainable phenomenon in laying in an urban field staring at the sky... feet to feet... feeling double my size, thanking Mother Nature, the wind and sky answering back with a perfect, hallow circle in the clouds above. It was on this day Rain Walker was born!
